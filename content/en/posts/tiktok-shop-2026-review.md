## Current State

Cross-border e-commerce in 2026 is brutally competitive. But competition doesn't mean no opportunity — it means you need to pick the right direction.

My current setup:
- **Indonesia**: Cash flow maintenance, no aggressive growth
- **US/Europe**: Main battlefield, building the full pipeline from product selection to content to ads

## Why TikTok Shop

1. Traffic dividends still exist, especially in Western markets
2. Content-driven, unlike Amazon's pure ad-spend model
3. Perfect for solo operators or small teams

## Lessons Learned

- Indonesia's review process is strict — one wrong product description and you're penalized
- Video content is king, but production costs add up
- Creator partnerships outperform self-made content

## My Framework

1. **Product Selection**: Skip trending items, find high-frequency necessities
2. **Content**: AI-assisted script generation to cut costs
3. **Ads**: Small budget testing, scale what works
4. **Review**: Weekly data review, kill unprofitable products fast

## What's Next

Building an AI-powered automation system. Goal: one person running multiple product lines with minimal manual work.
